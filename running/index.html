    Why I Run

    Running started as something I did to stay in shape. It's become something else entirely — a habit, a ritual, a way to think through hard problems and clear my head before the day starts.

    There's something about putting miles on your legs that makes everything else feel more manageable. Some runs are great. A lot of them aren't. I show up anyway.

    This page is my running log, synced automatically from Strava. Every run, every mile, tracked here.
